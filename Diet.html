A guide so you can have healthy Guinea Pigs


Guinea Pigs require unlimited hay for their digestive system to work properly and helps wear down their teeth.

Guinea Pigs also require grass-based pellets. Do not buy pellets that come with any types of mixes as that can cause health problems.
Pellets provide nutrients that hay can't. Baby, pregnant, or underweight guinea pigs should have more pellets.

Guinea Pigs cant produce their own vitamin C so you need to provide food and treats high in vitamin C, or it can lead to health problems if aren't provided enough vitamin C

Always provide clean, fresh water

Fruits make good occasional treats, but keep the amount small as they are high in sugar

Always have treats to give to the Guinea Pig to reward them

Guinea Pigs require vegetables everyday (Vegetable List Below)



There are many options, but these are the most popular choices. Some vegetables must be given occasionally.

  1. Romaine Lettuce
  2. Green Leaf Lettuce
  3. Red Leaf Lettuce
  4. Parsley
  5. Cilantro
  6. Kale
  7. Tomatoes
  8. Celery
  9. Cucumbers
  10. Bell Peppers
  11. Baby Carrots
