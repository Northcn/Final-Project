A guide so you can have healthy Guinea Pigs

  • Guinea Pigs require unlimited hay for their digestive system to work properly and helps wear down their teeth.
  • Guinea Pigs also require pellets. Do not buy pellets that come with any types of mixes as that can cause health problems.
