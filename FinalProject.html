This is my guide to help you become a good Guinea Pig owner

Guinea Pigs are cute little animals that make good pets if properly cared for. Many people think they are good starter pets or good pets for kids. This is NOT TRUE


Guinea Pigs can get expensive like every other pet, and they need proper care to live long, healthy lives.

Guinea Pigs are a long commitment, normally living from 5-7 years and sometimes longer
