Guinea Pigs need enough space to run around

Pet Store Cages are usually not big enough for a pair of Guinea Pigs


Cage size Requirements for 1-2 Guinea Pigs should be a minimum of 7.5 square
However, it's recommended that the cage be 10.5 square ft, but the bigger the better.
If you have more than 2 Guinea Pigs, you should size up the cage accordingly.

For each Guinea Pig you must have a bowl of food, water bottle, and hut for them alone as it can sometimes cause arguments.

Good Bedding Options

Good and Safe Bedding Options would be fleece as it's comfy and reusable
Hay and Paper Bedding are also good options
